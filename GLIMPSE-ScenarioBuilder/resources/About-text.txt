GLIMPSE is the GCAM Long-term Interactive Multi-Pollutant Scenario Evaluator, a graphical user interface that has been built for the GCAM model by researchers within the U.S. EPA's Office of Research and Development.  

GCAM is the Global Change Assessment Model, a human-Earth systems model that is publicly available via https://github.com/JGCRI/gcam-core. The lead developer for GCAM is the Joint Global Change Research Institute, a joint venture between the Pacific Northwest National Laboratory and the University of Maryland.
